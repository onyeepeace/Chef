Cake Menu

We have crafted a special menu of moist cakes which can be paired together with our decadent fillings and frostings for the best flavours.

Cakes can either be covered in buttercream or in fondant. The price of each cake will depend on the style and choice, as well as the size. Below is a list to guide you on the starting prices;

Buttercream cakes

  • 5" from £45 | serves 8-10 slices
  • 6" from £55 | serves 15 slices
  • 8" from £85 | serves 25 slices
  • 10" from £120 | serves 40 slices

Fondant cakes

  • 5" from £75 | serves 8-10 slices
  • 6" from £95 | serves 15 slices
  • 8" from £110 | serves 25 slices
  • 10" from £135 | serves 40 slices
