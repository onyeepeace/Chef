Reach out for your event

Cakes can either be covered in buttercream or in fondant. The price of each cake will depend on the style and choice, as well as the size. Below is a list to guide you on the starting prices;

Buttercream cakes

  • 5" from £45 | serves 8-10 slices
  • 6" from £55 | serves 15 slices
  • 8" from £85 | serves 25 slices
  • 10" from £120 | serves 40 slices

Fondant cakes

  • 5" from £75 | serves 8-10 slices
  • 6" from £95 | serves 15 slices
  • 8" from £110 | serves 25 slices
  • 10" from £135 | serves 40 slices

Flavours

  • Lovely Lemon | lemon sponge with lemon curd and buttercream
  • Chocolate truffle | a dark and beautiful chocolate sponge layered with dark chocolate ganache.
  • Chocolate & Praliné | the same chocolate sponge, filled with homemade hazelnut and almonds praliné buttercream (with orange zests in option)
  • Carrot Cake | a classic carrot sponge full of flavours and spices filled with vanilla buttercream.
  • Coffee & Hazelnut | A twist to the classic Coffee & Walnuts, a moist coffee sponge filled with homemade hazelnut praliné buttercream.
  • Almond, lemon and raspberry | a light almond sponge baked with raspberries and filled with lemon curd and buttercream. A must-have!
  • Maroccan Orange Blossom, Pistachio & Raspberry | a moist Orange sponge baked with fresh raspberries and filled with a homemade pistachio cream
